GO BAG (Emergency Preparedness Bag)

REMINDERS

Remember These Three Important Guides.

The GO BAG item should be checked and replenished every tree months.
Suriin ang laman ng GO BAG kada tatlong buwan at palitan ang mga laman nito na malapit nang masira.
The GO BAG should be stored properyly where it can be easily accessed in case of an emergency.
Ilagay ang GO BAG sa lugar na madali itong makita at makuha sa oras ng sakuna.
Coordinate with leader on community evacuation plan. Decide with the family on where to meet in case there is need to evacuate.
Makipag-ugnayon sa mga lider para sa plano sa pag-likas ng komunidad. Pag-usapan di sa pamilya kung saan tutungo sa oras na kailangang lumikas.

CONTENTS OF A GO BAG

The Go Bag Contains Important Item Needed by the Family to Survive.

Important ducument in waterproof container.
Mahalagang dokumento sa selyadong lalagyan.
Flashlight, candles, matches and whistle.
Flashlight, kandila, posporo at silbato.
Radio with fresh and extra batteries.
Radyo na may bago at ekstrang baterya.
First aid kit with remedies for fever, LBM minor wound and pain, and maintenance medicines.
First aid kit, kasama ang panlunas sa lagnat,LBM,sugat at iba pa, at mga gamot na regular na iniinom ng kapamilya
Spare cash including coin.
Pera at barya.
Items for special needs of young and older members of the family, including persons with disabilities.
Mga gamit para sa pangangailangan at may kapansanan.
Easy-to-serve,ready-to-eat food enough for three days.
Pagkain na madaling ihanda, di na kailangan lutuin,sapat para sa tatlong araw.
Drinking water in sealed containers enough for three days.
Inuming tubig sa saradong lalagyan at sapat para sa tatlong araw.
Mobile phones, powerbanks and chargers.
Telepono,powerbank at charger.
Clothing raincoat, boots and sanitary supplies.
Damit, kapote, botas at mga sanitary supplies.
Sleeping bags or mats and blankets.
Sleeping bag o banig, at mga kumot.
Ropes, old newspaper and ecobags mage ot strong materials.
Lubid, lumang dyaryo at matitibay na lalagyan.
